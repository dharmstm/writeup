Who is dharmstm?

I'm Dharmendra Kumar, a cybersecurity professional specializing in offensive security, web application penetration testing, and vulnerability research. Currently interning as a Penetration Tester at Terra System Labs.

I document every machine I solve — not just to track progress, but to help the next person learn the methodology. Each writeup walks you through my full thought process: enumeration, exploitation, privilege escalation, and lessons learned.

Recognized in vulnerability disclosure programs by NASA, Ulta Beauty, and Dreamscape Networks, and have published two CVEs in OpenKM — CVE-2026-30502 (Reflected XSS) and CVE-2026-30503 (Stored XSS).
